Philipp Panov
Global Head of Structured Notes & Head of Russia
Philipp has been working in the financial market since 1993. Prior to joining ITI Capital as a Managing Director, he was responsible for team-building and sales in Uralsib Capital and Renaissance Capital, interacting with institutional, corporate, private (HNWI) clients. Since joining ITI Capital in 2018, Philipp has been developing the group’s business in Russia.
Daria Folomeeva
ITI Tech
Prior to joining ITI Tech as a co-founder and ITI Capital as a business development head, Daria has worked in PRYTEK|GFS and she launched the project on American stock market analysis in Russia together with Israeli company TipRanks Ltd. Now she is the official representative of the company. At ITI Tech and ITI Capital Daria is responsible for launching of the new projects together with ITI’s partners and for the development of internal fintech platforms and solutions of ITI group.

Daria has started her career as the head of the joint project between Dow Jones Int. Ltd. and PRIME business news agency. After that Daria has worked with the projects in the financial sector, fintech startups and investment companies.
